## How To Use

<Tip>

You must grant permissions to proceed with usage. For details, visit: <a href='#grant-permissions'>Grant Permissions</a>

</Tip>

<Steps>

  <Step title="In the input field and enter the text content">Nice to see you</Step>
  <Step title='Type the end symbol ">" followed by the language code'>
    Nice to see you >ko

    <Card
      icon="language"
      title="Search Language Codes"
      href="https://hellohelloworld.notion.site/2198c9d5e49680088e5bff3ed8a8d456"
    />

  </Step>
  <Step title='Press the shortcut key: control + ">"'>
    1. Nice to see you >ko

    2. Press control + ">"

    3. 만나서 반가워요

  </Step>
  <Step title="To insert text at a specific position and translate it, use the start symbol">
    ```txt
    Samsung diversified into various areas as Lee aimed to establish the company as a leader across multiple industries.

    >> Nice to see you >ko => 만나서 반가워요

    The business expanded into sectors such as insurance, securities, and retail.
    ```

  </Step>
