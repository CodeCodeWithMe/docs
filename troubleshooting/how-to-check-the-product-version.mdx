## Chrome Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Extension Options">
    Right click the extension icon and click the "Options" menu item.
    <img src="/assets/dt/images/4.png" width="50%" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Product Version">
    You can see the product version at the top of the options page.
    <img src="/assets/dt/images/5.png" width="80%" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
