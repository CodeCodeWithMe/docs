---
title: "How To manually update extension"
description: "You can manually update the extension."
icon: "puzzle-piece-simple"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open URL">
    chrome://extensions/
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search For Extension">
    Search for the extension you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Extension Detail">
    Click the "Details" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Developer Mode">
    Click the toggle switch to enable Developer mode.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Extension">
    1. Click the "Update" button to update the extension.
    2. Wait for the update to complete and turn off the Developer mode.

    <Tip>
        The extension has been updated, you can now use the latest version.
    </Tip>

    <img src="/images/d13.jpg" />

  </Step>
</Steps>
