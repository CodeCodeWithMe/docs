## Chrome Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Extension Options Page">
    Right click the extension icon and click the "Options" menu item.
    <img src="/images/dt/2.png" width="30%" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open License Tab">
    <img src="/images/dt/3.png" width="80%" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate License">
    1. Enter your license key

    2. Click the activate button

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lemon Squeezy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open URL">https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders/</Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email And Signin">
    <img src="/images/d7.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deactivate License">

    1. Select your order item
    2. Click the license
    3. Click the deactivate button
    4. Confirm the deactivation

    <Tip>
        The license has been deactivated.
        You can now activate the license on another device.
    </Tip>

    <img src="/images/d8.png" />

  </Step>
</Steps>
